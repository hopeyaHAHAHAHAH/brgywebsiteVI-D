Services

Agricultural Support and Development

  • Farming Assistance: Access to training programs on sustainable farming techniques, crop management, and pest control to help our farmers maximize their yields.
  • Community Gardens: Initiatives to establish and maintain community gardens, promoting food security and encouraging local produce.
  • Market Access: Support for farmers in accessing local markets and cooperatives to sell their produce, ensuring fair pricing and profitability.

Health Services

  • Health Center: Our barangay health center provides essential medical services, including check-ups, vaccinations, maternal and child health care, and emergency services.
  • Health Education Programs: Regular workshops and seminars on nutrition, disease prevention, and healthy living to promote overall community wellness.
  • Mobile Health Services: Outreach programs that bring health services directly to underserved areas within the barangay.

About Us

Nestled in the heart of San Pablo City, Laguna, Barangay VI-D is a dynamic and thriving community that proudly blends urban living with a strong sense of local identity. Known for its dedicated and close-knit residents, Barangay VI-D is committed to continuous growth, progress, and the enhancement of the quality of life for everyone. Although primarily urban, the barangay is rich in history and values that have been passed down through generations, contributing to its unique character within the city.

Our residents are actively involved in various community initiatives aimed at promoting social cohesion, economic development, and overall well-being. From health and wellness programs to educational workshops, recreational activities, and local business support, Barangay VI-D serves as a hub for innovation and collaboration. We take pride in our ability to bring people together through shared experiences and a collective vision for a better future.
